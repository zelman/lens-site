Lens

An AI associate for retained executive search.

Structured discovery on both sides of a senior hire — a shared object for better conversations, not a hiring verdict.

What it is

I built Lens as an AI associate for retained search. A recruiter authors a role lens through facilitated intake — the deep version of a kickoff conversation — and Lens reads candidates against it.

The result isn't a verdict. It's a shared object: something the recruiter, the client, and the candidate can all point at to make the conversation sharper.

How it's different

Constructive identity, not a hiring verdict.

Most tools screen people out. Lens is built to draw people out — discovery that surfaces who someone is, rather than a score that ranks them.
